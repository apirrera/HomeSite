About Me

Aspiring Software Engineer

New York, NY

In my previous professional life, I've always had an interest in technology and art. I had liked drawing from an early age and took art classes in college. I was in the IT sector as a Desktop Support Analyst. My favorite parts of my career were solving problems and interacting with people to find solutions. The two things had been separate planes for me. I realized there could be an intersection between my current career and my hobby, the middle plane between art and technology. I started to learn UX design and as well as the process to create it. I want to be able to create websites and phone applications that I've designed.

I consider the values of consistency to be the most important. It's okay to fail and be disappointed that a vision doesn't match skill level, but it's best to enjoy the journey and any 1 centimeter of improvement. It's that repeated effort that's the most valuable. It's also best to only compare yourself to your past self. It's about beating the past you.

In the future, I want to create something meaningful in the world. I want to improve my digital art skills and programming ability. I want to use my art and technology skills to create a wonderful intersection.
